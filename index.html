About Me

Numbers by day,
stories by night.

I'm a Data Scientist based in Kolkata with dual academic credentials — a BSc in Mathematics (Honours) from the University of Burdwan and a BS in Data Science and Applications from IIT Madras, one of India's most prestigious institutions.

My journey isn't linear. While building a rigorous understanding of statistics, machine learning, and data engineering, I was simultaneously growing a parallel life as a content creator — editing videos, building YouTube audiences, and learning firsthand what makes an idea stick with people.

That combination is rare, and it's what I bring to every project: the discipline of a mathematician, the curiosity of a data scientist, and the communication instinct of a storyteller with 6+ years behind the camera.
